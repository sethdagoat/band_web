About

Joining the band offers a unique opportunity to dive into the rich world of music, exploring advanced band literature from various historical periods. You'll not only perform in concerts, festivals, and parades but also get the chance to energize the crowd at pep band games for football, basketball, and playoffs. Whether you're honing your instrumental techniques in small ensembles, or taking part in sectional rehearsals, you'll grow as a musician in an encouraging and dynamic environment. Plus, if you're already involved in sports, there's flexibility for athletes to participate without missing out on their games.
